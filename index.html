About Us

Parrabi (pronounced: pah·rah·bee) is an up and coming hardware engineering hiring platform that allows engineers/aspiring engineers to practice their hardware knowledge and skills and prep for hardware engineering interviews. Parrabi is also dedicated to providing an interactive training and evaluation platform that companies can make use of in remotely assessing their candidates for hardware engineering roles. Our mission is to provide Access to All by providing crucial tips, mentorship, and real Hardware interview questions. We are continuously working with industry leaders to tailor our questions to match what interviewers can expect.
